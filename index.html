Goa 2030

The Vision, Defined

Five commitments shaping the Goa I am determined to build — one venture, one decision, one year at a time.

Capital
Mobilise transformative investment across every corner of Goa
Channelling capital into ventures that build lasting wealth, create infrastructure and establish Goa as a premier investment destination in India.
Employment
Create thousands of skilled, dignified jobs for Goa's people
Building ventures that don't just generate profit but generate purpose — careers in technology, clean energy and knowledge industries rooted in Goa.
CleanTech
Power Goa entirely through clean, renewable energy
Installing solar, green hydrogen and sustainable infrastructure that protects Goa's coastline, reduces carbon footprint and leads India's green transition.
Startups
Mentor and fund the next generation of Goan founders
Providing capital, connections and conviction to ambitious entrepreneurs building from Goa — turning the state into a startup ecosystem others look up to.
Education
Reach millions of students through transformative EdTech
Democratising access to world-class education across India's tier-2 and tier-3 cities — because where you are born should never limit how far you can go.
