About Jayesh

Engineer by training.
Builder by calling.

Jayesh Parab is a Goa-based entrepreneur, investor, and engineer whose work sits at the intersection of technology, education, and community development. His career spans building products, backing founders, and building institutions that outlast any single company.

What began as engineering discipline has evolved into a broader conviction: that Goa's geography, culture, and talent are not limitations — they are competitive advantages waiting to be deployed.

Goa's natural wealth is irreplaceable. Every tech venture should amplify — not erode — the environment that makes Goa worth building in.

  • Coastal & marine tech investment

Policy & Infrastructure

The Mission

Goa 2030:
A seaport for ideas

By 2030, Jayesh Parab's mission is measurable: Goa ranked among India's top-5 startup ecosystems, with a generation of founders who built world-class companies without leaving the state they call home.

This is not a campaign. It is a construction project — built through education, infrastructure, policy, community, and patient capital deployed in the right hands at the right time.

"A seaport for ideas — not just ships. Goa has always connected India to the world. Now it is time for Goa's ideas to make that journey."

— Jayesh Parab · The Core Belief
