About Jayesh Parab

Entrepreneur, investor,
and Goa's loudest champion

Jayesh Parab is a serial entrepreneur and angel investor based in Goa, India. Over the last decade, he has built businesses, backed early-stage founders, and spent his career making the case that Goa's next economic era should be powered by Artificial Intelligence and startup innovation — not just tourism and mining.

His investment philosophy is grounded in deep regional empathy. Jayesh Parab looks for founders who understand Goa's specific geography, communities, and opportunities — and who are building solutions that can scale globally while creating genuine impact locally.

Beyond capital, Jayesh Parab is known as a hands-on mentor — sitting in strategy sessions, making introductions, challenging assumptions, and showing up for founders during the inevitable crises of early-stage growth. He is the kind of investor who answers the phone.

His broader mission is to make Goa one of India's top five startup destinations within a decade — through ecosystem building, policy advocacy, talent development, and the relentless belief that small states with the right leadership can compete with anyone.

Jayesh Parab cases each investment with the same lens: does this founder understand the problem deeply, is the technology defensible, and does success here create value for Goa's communities as well as its cap table?

Focus areas

Where Jayesh Parab invests

Six technology categories where Goa's natural advantages intersect with transformative AI opportunity — each selected for local depth and global scalability.

AI & Machine Learning

Startups building intelligent systems that learn, adapt, and create measurable outcomes — especially in sectors unique to Goa's economy and geography.

Sustainable Tourism Tech

AI tools that make Goa's tourism more personalised, efficient, and ecologically responsible — products exportable to beach destinations worldwide.

Healthcare AI

Personalised medicine, diagnostics, and health management platforms leveraging Goa's strength as a medical tourism destination and research base.

Smart Agriculture

Precision farming AI, crop disease prediction, and digital market platforms connecting Goa's farmers directly with premium buyers across India.

Ocean & Marine Tech

AI for fisheries management, maritime safety, ocean health monitoring, and aquaculture — Goa's blue economy made smarter and more sustainable.

Fintech & Inclusion

Financial products built for gig workers, fisherfolk, and informal entrepreneurs — AI-powered credit, savings, and insurance for real Goa.
